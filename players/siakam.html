PASCAL SIAKAM

Pascal Siakam was drafted in the 1st round in the 2016 NBA Draft with the 27th pick by the Toronto Raptors. In his rookie season he played in the G-League, with the Raptors 905, with his buddy Vanvleet. He led that team to a championship and won the Finals MVP. Siakam then became a huge part of the 2019 championship run, being the second leading scorer for the Raptors in the playoffs behind Finals MVP Kawhi Leonard. In that same year he also won the NBA MIP (Most Improved Player) award. In-between that season Siakam agreed to sign a 4 year deal worth $130 million. Following that successful year Siakam then became the number one option for the team, as superstar Kawhi Leonard left to go to LA. In Siakam’s first year being the number one option he made the NBA All Star Game, aswell as the ALL-NBA First Team. Pascal Siakam’s career highs are, 44 Points, 19 Rebounds, 12 Assists, 5 Steals and 4 Blocks.
